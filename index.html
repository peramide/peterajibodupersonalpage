About Me

I am a First Class Honours graduate in Industrial Chemistry from the University of Ibadan with a passion for multidisciplinary drug discovery research.

My undergraduate research explored the therapeutic potential of natural products and their wound-healing activity, which strengthened my interest in health-related material design. I have some hands-on analytical experience during my internship at the National Agency for Food and Drug Administration and Control (NAFDAC), Lagos, and through my current role as a Graduate Research Intern at the University of Abuja., I solve problems at the intersection of Science and Technology.
